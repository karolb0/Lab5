Student

The "Student" page in the EMS streamlines academic tasks, offering tools to view faculty, courses, update personal details, and register for classes efficiently.


Courses

Business

A business course covers essential topics like management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship. It teaches students practical skills and theoretical knowledge to succeed in various professional settings.

Biology

A biology course covers the study of living organisms, including cell biology, genetics, ecology, and physiology. Students learn about the natural world's diversity, interactions, and underlying processes, preparing them for careers in healthcare, research, and conservation.

Computer Science

A computer science course teaches algorithms, programming, and computer systems. Students learn problem-solving skills and software development techniques. Graduates can pursue careers in software engineering, data analysis, and cybersecurity.

Music

A music course covers theory, history, and performance across various genres and cultures. Students explore melody, harmony, rhythm, and cultural contexts. Through practical exercises and listening sessions, they develop creativity and critical thinking skills. Graduates pursue careers in performance, education, production, and research.

Nursing

A nursing course teaches patient care, medical procedures, and healthcare ethics. Students learn clinical skills and gain practical experience through rotations. Nursing education prepares students for roles as registered nurses (RNs) in diverse healthcare settings.
